Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

Welcome to FinanceTerux! Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our personal finance tracker.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: When you sign up, we collect your email address to create and verify your account.
  • Financial Transactions: We store the transactions you add, update, or delete within your account, including transaction names, amounts, categories, and dates.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account after verifying your email address.
  • To allow you to add, update, delete financial transactions.
  • To provide personalized finance tracking services.

3. Account Deletion

You can delete your account permanently at any time. Upon deletion, all your personal information, including your email and all associated transaction data, will be permanently deleted from our Firebase database.

4. Data Security

We use Firebase's secure infrastructure to store your data safely. We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your information to any third parties. Your data is used solely for providing you with FinanceTerux services.

6. Email Communication

We may send you emails for account verification and important service-related updates. You will not receive marketing or promotional emails from us unless you explicitly opt-in.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or through the app.

8. Contact Form Submissions

When you reach out to us through our Contact Us page, we use a third-party service called Formspree to handle your form submissions. Your submitted message (including your email address) is sent to us securely via Formspree, and we will respond to your inquiry directly via email. Formspree helps manage and process the contact form data efficiently.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.

Thank you for trusting FinanceTerux!
